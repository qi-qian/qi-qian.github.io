Biography

I joined Alibaba in 2015. Before that, I obtained the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Michigan State University in August 2015, under the supervision of Dr. Rong Jin .

I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from Nanjing University in June 2008 and June 2011, respectively. As a master student, I joined the LAMDA group and my supervisor is Dr. Zhi-Hua Zhou.

Research Interests

Machine learning: Online learning, two-sided matching, distance metric learning, multiple clustering

Computer vision: Fine-grained visual categorization, RGB-D image segmentation, feature learning
